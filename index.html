Additional Skills: A Quick Glance

Apart from the things I've learned from team projects building rockets and designing planes, here are some miscellaneous skills I've learned from internships, side hobbies, and more.

Data Analytics & Machine Learning

At the U.S. Naval Research Laboratory (NRL), I crunched hyperspectral imagery data and learned unsupervised (kPCA & manifold) and reinforcement (DQN & PPO) learning.

Public Speaking

I love orating my thoughts in front of crowds, whether that be as former 2021 Class Council VP or making videos on my Youtube channel.

Scientific Research

I'm currently conducting research on vertical-takeoff-and-landing (VTOL) aircraft for NRL and as my senior research project at TJHSST.

Model Building

From 1/48-scale 500-piece Tiger 1E models to a flying origami F-15C, I like to work with my hands and build models of engineering marvels.

Writing

I was never a big reader, but I've learned to express myself and stories through writing. Currently participating in NaNoWriMo!

Cybersecurity

Complete beginner, but I want to learn ethical hacking. All I've done so far is install and watch tutorials on Kali Linux.
